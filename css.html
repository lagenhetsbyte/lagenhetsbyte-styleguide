Do:

  • Place css declarations in alphabetical order. This is to easily find what you are looking for, and to eliminate the risk of duplication.
  • Use so called ”egyptian brackets”.
  • Indent by 4 spaces.

Nesting

Nesting is a expensive operation. Always try to find a direct selector. .some-list__item is always to prefer instead of .some-list > div + ul li.
